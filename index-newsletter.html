HTML Newsletter

As a group of 3, we "pair programmed" The Family Flyer, an HTML email newsletter to encourage families, especially those with younger children, to travel together.

After our image header, our first feature was our destination of the moment: Paris, France. The button routes the user to an external article about the City of Lights.

    This section is followed by 3 areas of content:
  • Highlighted social media accounts of traveling families
  • Hopeful parent hacks for flying with young children
  • Helpful tips from the Pla family on how to turn credit card points into a discounted (or free) vacation

We have embedded links to real social media accounts and external web articles.

As our first project accomplished via pair programming, it was a challenging, yet rewarding pleasure to prepare this for our Cohort-mates!
